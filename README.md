<h1 align="center">
 Budget App - Manage Your Finances with Ease
</h1>

<h3 align="center">
 Web Application
</h3>


<br><br>

<p align="justify">
The Budget App is a user-friendly web application that enables you to keep track of your finances by creating budgets and monitoring your expenses. Built with ReactJS, this app provides an intuitive and easy-to-use interface that helps you manage your money effectively.
<br><br>
With the Budget App, you can:
- <b>Create budgets:</b><br> Set your monthly, weekly, or yearly budgets according to your income and expenses. The app provides an easy-to-use form that allows you to enter your budget details quickly and easily.

- <b>Add expenses:</b><br> Add your expenses and categorize them according to your budget. The app provides a variety of pre-defined categories such as groceries, transportation, entertainment, and more, to help you organize your expenses efficiently.

- <b>View expense structure:</b><br> The app provides a clear and concise view of your expense structure, allowing you to analyze your spending habits and identify areas where you can cut back.

- <b>Track your progress:</b><br> The Budget App allows you to track your progress towards your budget goals. The app provides an overview of your spending history and shows how much of your budget you have spent so far.

- <b>Customize your settings:</b><br> The app allows you to customize your settings according to your preferences. You can set reminders for your expenses, customize your expense categories, and set your preferred currency.
</p>

<p align="justify">
The Budget App is designed to simplify your financial management and help you achieve your financial goals. It is secure, easy to use, and offers a range of features that enable you to track your finances effectively. Whether you are trying to save money, pay off debt, or simply manage your expenses, the Budget App is the perfect tool to help you get started.
<br>
So, if you are looking for an effective and easy-to-use budgeting tool, try out the Budget App today!
</p>
